# IBS Overlap
Source: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3093012/

There is a significant overlap between Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) and SIBO. Studies suggest that a substantial proportion of patients diagnosed with IBS may actually have underlying SIBO, particularly those with diarrhea-predominant IBS (IBS-D). Symptoms of both conditions are highly similar, including bloating, altered bowel habits, and abdominal discomfort.
